What are our treaties?

Our treaties are ideas that at least three of four countried of the UNM have agreed upon. These can range from Black Markets to Terrorism.

-----------------------------------------------

So how does a treaty get signed and what does it need?

Well a treaty has to have at least 3/4 signatures and votes to approve. And each one must deal with a serious problem such as Terrorism, Slave Owning, Illegal Weapons, etc.
But what makes them a treaty is how much the writer pours into it. Without the writer actually caring and wanting to do something about that subject, the treaty is just a piece of unwanted paper..

The Black Market Agreement

By signing this all countries agree that lethal weapons that do 9+ damage can only be legally sold on the black market (location not yet determined). Sales should be private and discreet. On the black market you can hire mercenaries, sell players, buy and sell weapons of mass destruction, and host currency giveaways.

Signatures 4/4
